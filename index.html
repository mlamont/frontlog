A Frontlog is an ordered list... of process improvements ("Try stuff.")... managed by a Scrum Master.
Frontlog.eth ("Out loud.") is a collection of Frontlogs... from teams across the world... managed by a Scrum Master Community. ("With friends.")

Manage what you do. Together.

Primary utility: "the effort"... by the Scrum Master (or Agile Coach or dual-hat Scrum Master)
  • much like a Backlog helps with the craft of Product Ownership,
    a Frontlog helps with the craft of Scrum Mastery
  • the level of effort is reflected in
    the level of quality of the Frontlog Items
  • Frontlog Items... check out the examples!
Secondary utility: "the output"... for the Community (of Agilists)
  • much like inspecting Backlogs enables cross-org coordination & adaptation of product improvements
    inspecting Frontlogs enables cross-org coordination & adaptation of process improvements
  • the breadth of high quality experiments through tactical documentation enhances
    the depth of purposeful connections inspiring further experimentation
  • further experimentation... check out the examples!
Tertiary utility: "the chatbot"... from our plans of, and to inform, our Agility (AI for Agile)
